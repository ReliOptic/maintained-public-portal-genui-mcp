## Completed

- Gov24 JA-code concepts are mapped as internal reference data, not exposed as
  new public MCP inputs.
- `DataSection` is part of the public benefit-search response schema and JSON
  Schema export.
- Daejeon Yuseong native lookup is available for `LAWD_CD` and `zscode`.
- Fixture-backed regional evidence sections are attached beside benefit
  candidates and are not ranked as benefits.
- Regional providers cover apartment rent transactions, resident population,
  parking, CCTV, and dong-level population.
- EV charger evidence is disclosed as unavailable until on-demand proxy and
  failure-disclosure behavior exists.
- Korean-law integration remains parked behind the explicit ADR-0022 decision
  gate; no legal text, OC key, or live legal MCP call is in the runtime or tests.
- Adapter discovery is exposed as `resource://adapters/v1`, so demos can show
  five fixture-backed scheduled adapters, the unavailable EV live path, and the
  parked korean-law path without changing `searchBenefits` semantics.
- The end-to-end demo scenario is now a newlywed/freelancer move to Daejeon
  Yuseong with benefit candidates plus regional evidence sections.

## Parked

- `korean-law-evidence` disabled skeleton and citation-verification review hook:
  waiting for explicit upstream acceptance of ADR-0022 Option A, B, or C.
- Live data.go.kr refresh and on-demand adapters: waiting for server-side proxy,
  key handling, and failure-disclosure design.
- EV charger live availability: waiting for the same on-demand proxy/failure
  disclosure infrastructure.

## Release Blockers

  regional sections are deterministic fixtures and must be labeled that way.
- `korean-law-mcp` must not be enabled without an accepted role decision and a
  server-side credential boundary.
